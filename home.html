Geotechnical and Materials Engineers

Reuling Associates, Inc. is a Baltimore-based small business specializing in construction testing and inspection, and geotechnical engineering. Since our incorporation in 1993, we have successfully completed several noteworthy projects in the Baltimore area.

Our personnel have vast experience on all types of construction projects including schools, hospitals, retail, office buildings, retirement homes, parking garages, even golf courses.

Our company growth has been fueled by repeat business with many of Baltimore's quality oriented contractors, developers, and owners including Whiting Turner Contracting Company, Barton Mallow Construction Company, Oak Contracting Company, Baltimore County Public Schools, Harford County Public Schools, Carroll County Public Schools, The Johns Hopkins University and Hospital, and many others.

We are committed to providing quality testing and inspection services utilizing qualified and competent technicians, backed up by an experienced and responsive office staff.
